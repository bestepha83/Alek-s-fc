Volleyball Team Players

Ben Stephan

Volleyball Team Manager

Our fearless leader, Ben only started his managing career to put on his resume. Turns out he's got a real knack for bossing people around. As a sturdy hitter, he leads from the front line, setting an example for his underlings. Some historians suspect William Shakespeare himself based his work off of Ben's pre-game motivational speeches.

Sierra Wohlford

"The Wolf"

Volleyball Team Coach

Sierra is known by few but feared by all. As the control tower of the team, Sierra's power sets are famous in the volleyball world. Rumor has it she's never missed a set in her entire life. Like poker, you do not want to play against Sierra's serve, she only deals aces.

Alek Arpe

"The Pipe Layer"

Volleyball Team Mascot

From his humble beginnings in Seattle, Washington, Alek Arpe has risen the ranks as the star player of his very own volleyball team. Alek may not the strongest, most athletic, most agile, or adept in any way towards the volleyball sport. But doggon it he will leave it all on the court every match. Alek's signature shot is the backrow pipe, a move he's familiar with on and off the court. He is the heart and soul of the team: neither can survive without the other.

Kieran Barker

"Special K"

Volleyball Team Player

While his toe may be totaled, never underestimate the sheer power of Kieran Barker. Conventionality was never part of the question for Kieran. Always prowling at the net, you never know when those massive quads will spring him up into the sky for a monstrous block sending the ball back into his pitiful opponent's face. His catch phrase is "Oh, very educational."

Dominic Baciocco

Volleyball Team Player

As the token frat boy in the team, his charming demeanor never ceases to amaze. But even better is his quality as an outside hitter. Legend has it, one of his spikes literally killed a dude. Outside of volleyball, you can catch Dom eating dinner or playing dye with his little and grandlittle.

Holly Faust

Volleyball Team Marketing Chair and Star Bench Warmer

Our most talented bench warmer, Holly's enthusiasm always provides results, both on and off the court. Her signature shot is her underhand serve, which catches her opponents off-guard by the loft and velocity it rains down. You will not find a better underhand server or bench shit-talker in the country.

Ford

"F-Bomb"

Volleyball Team Reinforcement

Number 4 in command, Ford brings a lot to the table. As the rookie of the team, he has a lot to prove, but if anybody was ever up to the task, by golly it's Ford. In pre-season practices, Ford was exceeding expectations, giving established star players a run for their money. The volleyball world is excited to see what he does next.
