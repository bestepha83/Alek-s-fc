About Ramona Sports

Our History

Born from the streets of Ramona Dr, this incredible sports club has partipated in many intramural sports. Since its conception back in Fall 2021, Ramona Sports Club (RSC) has an impressive track record of victories across the board. RSC is committed to performance and excellence in all activities.
